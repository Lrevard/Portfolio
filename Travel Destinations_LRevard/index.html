    Steps to Prepare for Your Trip

    1. Book your park tickets & flight.
    2. Reserve your hotel.
    3. Pack comfortable clothes and shoes for walking throughout the day.
    4. Check weather daily and dress or pack accordingly
    5. Download the official disney world app for ride wait times, and park maps.
    6. Create a Itinerary of which parks and attractions you would like to visit.
    7. Get Excited because your about to go to Disney World!

    Travel Glossary

    Guests
    Disney visitors are referred to as guests.
    Cast Members
    Disney Employees
    Lightning Lane
    For more popular rides, guests are able to purchase access to lightning lanes when they purchase their park tickets, allowing to bypass regular standby lines.
    Magic Bands
    Magic bands at disney are a wearable device, capable of being used as your ticket, your room key, and if your on a dining plan . your capble of purchasing food with it.
    Park Hopper
    a Park hopper is a type of ticket that allows you to visit more than one park on the same day.
    Photo Pass
    A photo pass allows you to take part in disney's own magical pictures. Photographer Cast members are placed throughout each park that allow guests to take Magical photos with enhancemnts or surprise special effects.
